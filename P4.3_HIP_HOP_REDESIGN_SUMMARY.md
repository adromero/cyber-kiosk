## What Changed

### Background
- **Before:** Black (#1A1A1A) - similar to Cyberpunk
- **After:** Purple gradient (#2A1A4A ‚Üí #1A0F2E) with spray paint radial overlays
- **Impact:** Creates completely different atmosphere

### Color Palette
- **Before:** 3 colors (gold, orange-red, turquoise) - too similar to Cyberpunk
- **After:** 5+ vibrant colors:
  - Gold Bright (#FFED4E)
  - Purple (#9D00FF)
  - Orange (#FF6B00)
  - Cyan (#00E5FF)
  - Lime (#ADFF2F)
- **Impact:** Much more colorful and energetic

### Typography
- **Before:** Oswald + Bebas Neue (similar weight to Cyberpunk)
- **After:**
  - Lilita One (bubble letters, graffiti-style)

- **Impact:** Completely different typeface personality

### Borders & Shadows
- **Before:** 3px borders, simple shadows
- **After:** 5-6px borders with 8-12px drop shadows
- **Impact:** Bold boombox aesthetic vs Cyberpunk's thin lines

### Visual Effects (NEW)

1. **Graffiti Drip Separator**
   - Rainbow gradient bar with paint drip effects

2. **Boombox Speaker Grilles**
   - Repeating line patterns on widgets
   - Creates cassette/speaker aesthetic

3. **Cassette Tape Track Lines**
   - Decorative patterns on widget headers
   - Reinforces old-school audio theme

4. **Spray Paint Overlays**
   - Crosshatch pattern on body background
   - Subtle texture like graffiti wall

### Animations (NEW)

1. **Boom-Bap Bounce Loading**
   - Bouncing animation with color shift
   - Cycles through gold ‚Üí purple ‚Üí orange ‚Üí cyan

   - Replaces subtle glow

### Text Shadows
- **Before:** Simple 2-layer shadows
- **After:** Multi-color layered shadows (purple + orange + gold)
- **Impact:** Bold graffiti-style depth
